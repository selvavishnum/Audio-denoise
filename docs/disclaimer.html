2. No Guarantee of Results; Variability of Performance

Processing results vary. The noise-cancellation, voice-enhancement, and vocal and music separation capabilities of the App are designed to improve the perceptual quality of audio and video recordings. Many users find that the App significantly reduces background noise and improves the clarity of voice recordings. However, the degree of improvement that you experience will depend on many factors, including the nature and intensity of the background noise (for example, stationary noise such as HVAC hum is generally easier to reduce than transient noise such as crowd chatter, passing traffic, or intermittent mechanical sounds); the proximity of the microphone to the source; the quality, sample rate, and bit depth of the recording; the characteristics of the device microphone; the processing capabilities of your device; whether any audio or video compression has already been applied to the source file; and the acoustic properties of the recording environment.

No guarantee of noise-free output. We do not guarantee that processing your recording through the App will eliminate all background noise, that the output will be perceptually indistinguishable from a professionally engineered recording, or that the output will meet any particular objective or subjective standard of audio quality. Some recordings will benefit greatly; others may show only modest improvement; and in some cases — for example, recordings where the background noise is so severe that it dominates the signal, where the recording quality is very low, or where the noise characteristics fall outside the frequency ranges best handled by the processing pipeline — the App may not produce a significant improvement, or may in some cases introduce artefacts such as mild reverberation, frequency colouration, or transient distortion. This is inherent in the nature of audio signal processing and does not indicate a defect in the App.

Vocal and music separation limitations. The vocal and music separation feature is intended to produce separate voice-dominant and instrument-dominant tracks from a mixed recording. It is not a professional stem-separation or music-production tool. The quality of separation depends on the complexity of the mix, the degree of overlap between vocal and instrumental frequencies, and the original recording quality. Output tracks may contain residual cross-contamination, artefacts, or quality loss, especially in dense musical arrangements. Results should not be used for professional music production, broadcast, or content distribution without critical evaluation and professional post-processing.

Evaluate before relying. Before using the output of the App for any purpose where quality matters — including publication, distribution, broadcast, legal proceedings, accessibility provision, or professional or commercial use — you should listen critically to the output and determine whether it meets your requirements. 4. On-Device Processing and Data Handling

Your audio stays on your device — on the default path. The App is designed around the principle that audio and video processing should occur on your device. By default, all noise cancellation, voice enhancement, vocal and music separation, and related processing is performed by software and machine-learning models that run within the App on your Android device. Under this default on-device path, your recordings and imported media files are never transmitted to us, to our servers, or to any third-party server for processing, and we do not receive, store, or have access to the content of media that you process. This is a design choice, not merely a policy statement, and it means that the substantive contents of your recordings are outside our control, custody, or knowledge.

The optional cloud path. The App also makes available one optional cloud-based processing engine: integration with the ElevenLabs Audio Isolation API. This engine is not selected by default and is never engaged automatically. It is available only if you expressly navigate to the relevant setting, select the ElevenLabs processing engine, and supply your own ElevenLabs API key. If you do so and then submit audio for processing through that engine, the audio you choose to process is transmitted to ElevenLabs' servers under your own credentials and is handled in accordance with ElevenLabs' terms of service and privacy policy. We are not responsible for ElevenLabs' handling of that audio. You should review ElevenLabs' policies before enabling this feature, particularly if the audio you intend to process contains personal data about third parties.

Your responsibility for lawful processing. The fact that the App processes audio locally does not affect your obligations under applicable law. If you record or process audio or video that captures the voice, image, or personal information of other people, you are solely responsible for ensuring that you have the authority, consent, and lawful basis to make and process that recording. Accuracy of the privacy statement. The statement "your audio never leaves your phone" is an accurate description of the default on-device processing path. It does not apply to any audio you deliberately transmit through the ElevenLabs cloud path, to any audio files you manually share or export to other applications or services, or to any content you upload to other platforms.
