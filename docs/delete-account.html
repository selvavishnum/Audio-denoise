Delete Your NoiseClear Account & Data

Applies to the NoiseClear Android app (developer contact: noiseclear.review@gmail.com).

You can request deletion of your NoiseClear account and its associated data at any time using either method below.

Option 1 — Delete in the app (fastest)

  1. Open NoiseClear.
  2. Go to the Settings tab.
  3. In the Account section, tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm. Your account and associated data are deleted immediately.

If you are asked to sign in again first, sign out and sign back in, then repeat the steps (this is a security check required before deletion).

Option 2 — Request by email

Send an email to noiseclear.review@gmail.com with the subject “Delete my account” from the email address associated with your account. We will verify and delete your account and associated data within 30 days.
