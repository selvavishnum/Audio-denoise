4. Description of the NoiseClear Service

What the App does. NoiseClear is an artificial-intelligence application for Android (package identifier com.noiseclear.app) that processes audio and video media to reduce or remove unwanted noise, to enhance speech and vocal clarity, and to separate a mixed recording into its constituent components. The App is designed to help you obtain cleaner, more intelligible recordings from material that you record within the App or import from your device. Its principal functions are:

  • Audio and video noise cancellation, which attenuates background noise such as ambient hum, hiss, wind, traffic, keyboard sound, and similar interference present in a recording or in the audio track of a video;
  • Voice enhancement, which seeks to improve the clarity, presence, and intelligibility of spoken or recorded voice; and
  • Vocal and music separation, which attempts to isolate vocal content from instrumental or musical accompaniment, or otherwise to split a recording into separate stems.

These functions are made available through controls within the App and may be applied to media you capture using the device microphone or camera, or to compatible files you import from device storage. Processed output may then be exported and saved back to your device.

On-device processing is the default. Most processing performed by the App takes place locally, on your device, using models and software that run on the device itself. On this default on-device path, the audio and video you process are not uploaded to us or to any third party, and we do not receive, store, or have access to the content of the media you process. This is what we mean by our description that, on the on-device path, your audio never leaves your phone. Because this processing is performed by your device, the speed, quality, and availability of these functions depend on your device's hardware, available memory and storage, operating-system version, and overall condition, and results will vary accordingly.

Optional ElevenLabs cloud path. As an alternative to on-device processing, the App offers an optional cloud-based processing path that uses the ElevenLabs Audio Isolation API.
